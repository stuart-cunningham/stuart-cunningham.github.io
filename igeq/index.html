In-Game GEQ (Game Experience Questionnaire) Score Calculator

This page can be used to calculate scores for the I-GEQ devised by IJsselsteijn et al., who explain that it was created "...for assessing game experience at multiple intervals during a game session, or play-back session" [1]. Average scores are produced for each of the seven components. The greater the score in each component, the greater the manifestation of that component in the player's experience.
